# IELTS Marathon Bot: Finalization Roadmap To ensure the system is production-ready and provides a premium experience for paid students, I recommend the following three phases: ## Phase 1: The "Trust" Layer (Student Experience) * **Receipt Upload Flow:** A dedicated screen where students upload their proof of payment. * **Pending State:** A "Verification in Progress" view that keeps the student engaged (perhaps with a sample lesson or FAQ) while the admin reviews the receipt. * **Error Handling:** Clear messaging for failed payments or incorrect file uploads. ## Phase 2: The "Persistence" Layer (Academic History) * **Student Portfolio:** A centralized "Library" of the student's own work. Access to every previously graded essay and speaking recording. * **Milestone Rewards:** Visual celebrations (badges/animations) when a student hits a 7-day streak or reaches a specific Band Score improvement. ## Phase 3: The "Control" Layer (Admin Operations) * **Content Uploader UI:** A simple interface to upload a PDF, MP3, and Homework JSON for a new lesson, which then automatically populates the Supabase storage and database. * **User Management:** A way to search for specific students, manually adjust their points, or reset their streaks if needed. --- **Current Status:** Core Logic & Visuals Complete. **Suggested Next Action:** Design the "Receipt Upload & Pending" flow.
